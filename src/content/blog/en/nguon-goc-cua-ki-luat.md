## 1. Concept

Discipline is based on two types:

- **Habit**: the way we do things regularly, hard to change
- **Force**: everyone has to follow the harsh rules to keep safety 

## 2. Rule

### The habit

To form, **Habit** is based on two machanism:

- Reward
- Repeating

**Examples:**

- Why do we usually brush our teeth?
- Why do we drink coffee every morning?
- Why do we turn on the TV after we go home from the school? 

→ Because every Habits always has rewards that easy to see, easy to achieve, easy to maintain.

**Thanks to Dopamine - Hormone Motivation since:**

- Training Axon, Myelin and Synapse everytime we get rewards
- Training the Stability, Speed and Connection of the Mind everytime we get rewards 

:::result[Result]
Habits aren't only formed from the Repeating, they also are supported by the Rewards that easy to see, easy to achieve, easy to maintain
:::

### The Forcing

To form, Forcing is based on this machanism: 

- **Anterior Mid Cingulate Cortex (aMCC)** - the part of brain helps forcing ourselve

- Why do I wake up soon to study even through I'm sleepy? 
- Why do I still work even through I don't like?
